Welcome to Home Sphere Empowering Your World with Home Sphere

Welcome to Home Sphere, an innovative app crafted with the Ionic framework and integrated with Firebase. Our application brings the future of home monitoring to your fingertips, harnessing the power of advanced Arduino sensors.

 Real-Time Sensor Integration

Our array of sensors from Arduino seamlessly connects with Firebase's Realtime Database, offering you:

  • Instantaneous Updates: Get real-time readings from sensors, ensuring you're always informed.
  • Accurate Monitoring: From temperature to motion detection, our sensors provide precise data.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy-to-navigate app design for efficient monitoring and control.
 Sensors At Work

Explore how each sensor contributes to your smart environment:

  • Temperature Sensor: Keeps your living space at the perfect temperature.
  • Humidity Sensor: Monitors moisture levels for comfort and health.
  • Pressure Sensor: Tracks atmospheric pressure, crucial for weather forecasting and indoor comfort.
  • Moisture Sensor: Ideal for plant care, ensuring your indoor greenery receives the right amount of water.
  • CO2 Sensor: Ensures the air you breathe is clean and safe, vital for maintaining healthy indoor air quality.
  • Gas Resistance Sensor: Detects harmful gases, playing a key role in preventing indoor air pollution.
  • Volatile Organic Compounds (VOC) Sensor: Monitors VOC levels, essential for identifying indoor air contaminants and ensuring a healthier living space.
  • Motion Sensors (PIR): Enhances security by detecting unexpected movements while you're on holiday
